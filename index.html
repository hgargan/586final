DRIVE SHENANDOAH

Skyline Drive runs along the ridge of the Shenandoah Mountains in Shenandoah National Park. This 105-mile-long road dips and dives between 2,000 and 4,000 feet as it takes sightseers over gaps, through tunnels and past meadows. Nearby, the world-famous Appalachian Trail follows a similar route.

To explore the route, click on the waypoints and drag the little yellow man on top of them to learn more about points of interest and explore this drive's amazing views.
