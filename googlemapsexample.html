EXPLORE SHENANDOAH

Skyline Drive runs along the ridge of the Shenandoahs in Shenandoah National Park. This 105-mile-long road reaches an elevation of nearly 4,000 feet as it passes over gaps, through tunnels and past meadows.

To explore the route, click on points on the map to learn about the drive's features and see some of its spectacular views.
